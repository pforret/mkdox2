# Sales process

How we move a prospect from first contact to signed contract.

## 1. Discovery call

- Goals: understand the problem, qualify fit, surface budget and timeline
- Use the [call notes template](../templates/call-notes.md)
- File the prospect under [Clients](../people/clients.md) with status `prospect`

## 2. Proposal

- Draft within 48 hours of discovery
- Reference past comparable engagements from [Decisions](../actions/decisions.md)

## 3. Follow-up

- Send the [follow-up email](../templates/follow-up.md) within 24 hours
- Add a follow-up [action](../actions/index.md) for 3, 7 and 14 days

## 4. Close

- Move the client status to `active`
- Kick off the [production workflow](production.md)
