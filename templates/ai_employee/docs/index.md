Welcome to the operating system for your business. This vault is the persistent brain that your AI assistant reads at the start of every session.

> Inspired by [Fraser Cottrell's Claude + Obsidian setup](https://x.com/sourfraser/status/2035454870204100810).

## How to use this vault

1. Edit [Memory](memory.md) — the onboarding doc for your AI employee.
2. Keep [People](people/index.md) up to date with clients and team.
3. File new work into [Actions](actions/index.md) and [Decisions](actions/decisions.md).
4. Document recurring playbooks in the [Library](library/index.md).
5. Drop call recordings into [Transcripts](transcripts/index.md) and let the AI process them.

See [Setup](setup.md) for wiring Claude, Obsidian and MCP connectors.
