# Onboarding

Welcome. Read through this page on day one — you'll be productive by the next meeting.

## Day one

1. Read the [Charter](../about/charter.md) and [Bylaws](../about/bylaws.md).
2. Skim the last three [meeting minutes](../meetings/index.md).
3. Browse [Decisions](../decisions/index.md) — the standing commitments you've inherited.
4. Look at active [Projects](../projects/index.md) and [Topics](../topics/index.md). Pick one or two that interest you.
5. Add yourself to [Members](index.md). Tell the [Secretary](roles.md) which topics you'll follow.

## First month

- Attend your first meeting as observer/full member (see [Calendar](../calendar.md)).
- Volunteer for one concrete piece of work.
- Ask questions in writing — the [Chair](roles.md) and the rest of the committee will answer.

## Conventions

- This site is editable by all members. When in doubt, edit and let the [Secretary](roles.md) know.
- Use plain language. Assume the next reader has not been in the room.
- Link liberally — every name, project and decision should resolve to its page.
