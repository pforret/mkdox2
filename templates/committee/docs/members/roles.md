# Roles

What each role does. The current holders are listed in [Members](index.md).

## Chair

- Sets the meeting [agenda](../meetings/template-agenda.md) (with input from members)
- Chairs meetings, keeps to time
- Represents the committee externally
- Ensures decisions are followed through

## Vice-chair

- Stands in for the Chair when needed
- Often takes a coordination role on a major [project](../projects/index.md)

## Secretary

- Convenes meetings (notice + agenda, _N_ days ahead — see [Bylaws](../about/bylaws.md))
- Takes [minutes](../meetings/template-minutes.md) and circulates them
- Maintains the [Members](index.md), [Meetings](../meetings/index.md) and [Decisions](../decisions/index.md) pages
- Custodian of this knowledge base

## Treasurer (if applicable)

- Keeps the books
- Prepares financial summaries for meetings
- Owns the [Finance topic](../topics/topic-finance.md)

## Members

- Attend meetings, prepare in advance
- Lead or contribute to [projects](../projects/index.md) and [topics](../topics/index.md)
- Vote on decisions

## Topic leads

A member can volunteer to lead a [topic](../topics/index.md). The lead is the go-to contact and keeps the topic page current.
