# Decisions

Append-only log of every formal decision the committee has taken. The voting record lives in the [meeting minutes](../meetings/index.md); this page is the searchable index.

## Numbering

Each decision gets an ID like `D-NNNN`, assigned in order. Once a decision is logged, it stays — supersessions reference the original.

## Index

| ID | Title | Status | Decided | Meeting |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [D-0001](0001-establish-decisions-log.md) | Establish a decisions log | Accepted | {CREATION_DATE} | [Kickoff](../meetings/2026/2026-04-22-kickoff.md) |

## When to record a decision here

- Any vote (consensus or formal).
- Any standing commitment that affects future meetings (a policy, a recurring action, a budget envelope).
- Any change to the [Charter](../about/charter.md) or [Bylaws](../about/bylaws.md).

## When *not* to

- Routine action items — those live on the relevant [project page](../projects/index.md).
- Discussion that didn't resolve — that stays in the [minutes](../meetings/index.md) only.
