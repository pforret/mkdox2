# Meetings

Agendas and minutes for every meeting, in reverse chronological order.

## Templates

- [Agenda template](template-agenda.md) — copy this for each new meeting
- [Minutes template](template-minutes.md) — copy this when taking notes

## Process

1. The [Secretary](../members/roles.md) creates a new meeting page from the [agenda template](template-agenda.md), at least _N_ days before the meeting (per [Bylaws](../about/bylaws.md)).
2. Members add agenda items via PR or message to the Secretary.
3. During the meeting, someone takes minutes directly into the page using the [minutes template](template-minutes.md).
4. Within _N_ days, minutes are reviewed and the meeting page is finalised.
5. New decisions get a corresponding entry in [Decisions](../decisions/index.md). New action items roll into the relevant [project](../projects/index.md) page.
