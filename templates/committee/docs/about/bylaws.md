# Bylaws

How we organise ourselves and make decisions.

## Membership

- **Composition:** _e.g. up to 9 elected members + 2 ex-officio_
- **Term length:** _e.g. 2 years, renewable once_
- **Election:** _how new members are chosen_
- See [Members](../members/index.md) for the current roster.

## Roles

The standing roles ([Roles](../members/roles.md) for descriptions):

- Chair
- Vice-chair
- Secretary
- Treasurer (if applicable)

## Meetings

- **Cadence:** _e.g. monthly, second Tuesday at 19:30_
- **Quorum:** _e.g. simple majority of members_
- **Convocation:** notice and agenda circulated _N_ days in advance
- See [Meetings](../meetings/index.md) and the [agenda template](../meetings/template-agenda.md).

## Decisions

- **By consensus** when possible.
- **By vote** when consensus is not reached:
    - Simple majority for routine matters
    - Two-thirds for charter / bylaw changes or financial commitments above _AMOUNT_
- Every formal decision is recorded in the [Decisions log](../decisions/index.md).

## Conflicts of interest

Members must declare conflicts and recuse themselves from the relevant decision. Recusals are noted in the meeting minutes.

## Amending these bylaws

Bylaw changes require:

1. A proposal circulated at least one full meeting in advance.
2. A two-thirds vote at the next meeting.
3. An entry in the [Decisions log](../decisions/index.md) referencing the proposal.
